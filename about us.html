WHAT DO WE DO?

Donating blood is simple thing to do, but save the most precious human life.It can make a big difference in someone's life.Basically you have to just take take up the survey followed by filling the donation form.Many volunteers have joined us in this social cause We make people aware of blood donations about how it will benefit them as well as the needy
Many people loose life because they don't get blood during operations.we organize donation camps and educate rural people of the same .We teach them ways how to identify the fake blood donation scams and the importance of blood donation You can be a part of the social cause by just donating your blood and giving them a chance to live.
